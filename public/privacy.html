Privacy Policy

How we handle your data on FemboyMap

Data Collection and Storage

When you view FemboyMap, we collect and store the following information:

Location Data

We display markers on the map that represent approximate locations shared by community members. These locations are stored as latitude and longitude coordinates in our database.

IP Address Hashing

For privacy protection, we store a salted SHA-256 hash of your IP address. This serves to:

  • Ensure your anonymity while browsing the map
  • Prevent abuse of the service
  • Maintain the integrity of the community map

The original IP address is never stored - only the irreversible cryptographic hash.

Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely for:

  • Displaying markers on the community map
  • Preventing spam and abuse
  • Maintaining service functionality

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties. All information remains within our secure database and is used exclusively for the operation of FemboyMap.

Data Retention

Marker location data and IP address hashes are retained indefinitely to maintain the community map. However, due to the hashed nature of IP addresses, individual users cannot be directly identified.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • IP address hashing for anonymity
  • Regular security assessments
